Welcome!

Hello and welcome to Carmelita's Bakery! Here, we offer cakes and other mexican baked goods that we can't wait to share with you!
While not a master baker just yet, Carmen is a passionate home baker who is enthusiastic and dedicated to learning more through the challenges that baking has to offer. Each creation is a labor of love, reflecting the rich and comforting flavors of traditional Mexican baking.

We're thrilled to have you here, experiencing the joy of homemade goodness. Thank you for being a part of this baking journey.


What do we offer?

Carmen primarily works with cakes, some cakes she has experience with include:

  • Flan Mexicano (Mexican Flan)
  • Pastel de Tres Leches (Three Milk Cake)
  • Pastel de Elote (Mexican Corn Cake)

However, Carmen is open to requests and is not limited to just Mexican cakes!
