About

Meet Carmen

Carmen is a home-based baker in Charlotte, NC. She has always had an interest in baking, but didn't start baking seriously until the past few years. Since then, she has been baking all kinds of cakes for close friends. What started as a hobby soon became a passion. With a lot of practice and confidence, Carmen is finally ready to share her skills and her baked goods beyond close friends and family. She hopes that you enjoy her baked cakes and goods!
